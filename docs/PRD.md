# Product Requirements Document (PRD): College Copilot
## Vision
Our vision is to create an innovative, AI-powered copilot that empowers college students to achieve academic success by providing instant, accurate, and personalized answers to their questions, thereby enhancing their learning experience and outcomes.

## Problem
College students often face numerous challenges in their academic journey, including:
* Difficulty in understanding complex concepts and subjects
* Limited access to teaching assistants, tutors, or professors outside of class hours
* Overwhelming amount of study materials, making it hard to find relevant information
* High stress levels due to uncertainty about assignments, exams, and grades
* Limited resources for students with disabilities or language barriers

Current solutions, such as textbooks, online forums, and tutoring services, often fall short in providing immediate, accurate, and personalized support, leading to frustration, decreased motivation, and poor academic performance.

## Solution
The College Copilot is an AI-driven platform that utilizes natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to provide instant, accurate, and personalized answers to students' questions. The platform will:
* Offer a user-friendly interface for students to ask questions, either through text or voice input
* Utilize a vast knowledge base of academic content, including textbooks, research papers, and online resources
* Employ AI-powered algorithms to analyze the question, identify relevant information, and provide a concise, accurate answer
* Continuously learn and improve its responses based on user feedback and interactions

## Target Users
Our primary target users are college students, including:
* Undergraduate and graduate students
* Students with disabilities or language barriers
* International students
* Students from diverse academic backgrounds and disciplines

## MVP Features
The minimum viable product (MVP) will include the following features:
1. **Question Input**: Users can ask questions through a text-based interface or voice input
2. **Knowledge Base**: Access to a comprehensive knowledge base of academic content, including textbooks, research papers, and online resources
3. **AI-Powered Answering**: The platform will utilize AI algorithms to analyze the question and provide a concise, accurate answer
4. **User Feedback**: Users can provide feedback on the accuracy and helpfulness of the answers
5. **Basic User Profile**: Users can create a basic profile to save their questions and answers for future reference

## Metrics
To measure the success of the College Copilot, we will track the following metrics:
1. **User Engagement**: Number of users, questions asked, and answers provided
2. **Answer Accuracy**: Percentage of accurate answers, as rated by users
3. **User Satisfaction**: User satisfaction ratings, based on feedback and surveys
4. **Retention Rate**: Percentage of users who return to the platform over time

## Roadmap
The development and launch of the College Copilot will follow this high-level roadmap:
1. **Research and Development** (6 weeks): Conduct market research, develop the AI-powered answering algorithm, and design the user interface
2. **MVP Launch** (12 weeks): Launch the MVP with basic features and a limited knowledge base
3. **Iteration and Improvement** (24 weeks): Gather user feedback, iterate on the platform, and expand the knowledge base
4. **Full Launch** (12 weeks): Launch the full version of the College Copilot, with all planned features and a comprehensive knowledge base

## User Stories
The following user stories will guide the development of the College Copilot:
1. **As a college student, I want to be able to ask questions and receive instant, accurate answers, so I can better understand complex concepts and subjects.**
2. **As a student with a disability, I want the platform to be accessible and provide accommodations, such as text-to-speech functionality, so I can use the platform easily.**
3. **As an international student, I want the platform to provide answers in multiple languages, so I can understand the content more easily.**
4. **As a user, I want to be able to provide feedback on the accuracy and helpfulness of the answers, so the platform can improve over time.**
5. **As a student, I want to be able to save my questions and answers for future reference, so I can review and study more effectively.**
